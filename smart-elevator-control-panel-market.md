<p><strong>The Smart Elevator Control Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Automatic Operation Panel</li><li>Selective Collective Operation Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart elevator control panel market features two primary types: Single Automatic Operation Panels and Selective Collective Operation Panels. Single Automatic Operation Panels allow users to input a destination with minimal intervention, usually stopping at every floor, providing convenience in low-rise buildings. In contrast, Selective Collective Operation Panels optimize elevator efficiency by grouping calls based on destinations, reducing wait times, and improving traffic handling in high-rise settings. Both types enhance user experience and operational efficiency in modern elevator systems.</p></p>
