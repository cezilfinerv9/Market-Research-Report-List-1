<p><p>The fertility treatments market encompasses various methods, primarily intrauterine insemination (IUI) and in vitro fertilization (IVF). IUI involves placing sperm directly into the uterus during ovulation to enhance the chances of fertilization. It's less invasive and typically recommended for couples with specific fertility issues. In contrast, IVF is a more complex procedure where eggs are retrieved, fertilized in a lab, and then implanted into the uterus. IVF is often used for a broader range of fertility challenges, providing higher success rates but at a greater cost and complexity.</p></p>
