<p><p>A centerless grinding machine is a type of grinding equipment that allows for precise and efficient grinding of cylindrical parts without the need for a fixture or support for the workpiece. The process involves placing the workpiece between two rotating wheels: a grinding wheel and a regulating wheel, which guides the part through the grinding process. This technique is widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for producing high-precision components.</p><p>The Centerless Grinding Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 1.24% during the forecast period.
