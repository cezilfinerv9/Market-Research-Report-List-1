<p><p>The ISO pumps market is primarily categorized into three types: centrifugal pumps, positive displacement pumps, and other specialized pumps. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to move fluids, making them efficient for high flow rates. Positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed volume of liquid and forcing it through the discharge, ideal for high-viscosity fluids. The "other" category encompasses various niche pumps, including diaphragm and screw pumps, each tailored for specific industrial applications and fluid characteristics, enhancing versatility in the market.</p></p>
