<p><p>Restoration anchor markets consist of three main types: </p><p>1. **Mechanical Restraining Anchors**: These provide stabilization to structures by resisting lateral forces, enhancing their integrity in adverse conditions. </p><p>2. **Mechanical Repair Anchors**: Designed for reinforcing and repairing existing structures, they help restore strength and fix damages. </p><p>3. **Helical Tie Anchors**: These screw-like anchors are driven into the ground to support structures, commonly used for added stability in foundation applications. Each type plays a critical role in structural restoration and enhancement.</p></p>
