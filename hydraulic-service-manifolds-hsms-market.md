The market primarily consists of two types: modular manifolds and single-piece manifolds. Modular manifolds offer flexibility and scalability, allowing for easy customization and expansion of hydraulic circuits. In contrast, single-piece manifolds are characterized by their robustness and compact design, providing a streamlined solution for specific applications where space and weight are critical considerations.</p></p>
