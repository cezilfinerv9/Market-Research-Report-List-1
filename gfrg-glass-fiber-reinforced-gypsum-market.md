<p><p>The GFRG (Glass Fiber Reinforced Gypsum) market comprises various types, primarily Ordinary Plaster and Flame Retardant Plaster. Ordinary Plaster is commonly used for general construction and finishing purposes due to its lightweight and ease of application. In contrast, Flame Retardant Plaster incorporates additives that enhance fire resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring enhanced safety measures, particularly in commercial and high-risk environments. Both types cater to distinct construction needs while leveraging the durability and versatility of GFRG materials.</p></p>
