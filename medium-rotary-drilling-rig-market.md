<p><p>The medium rotary drilling rig market is segmented into two main types: crawler and rubber-tired rigs. Crawler rigs, mounted on tracked chassis, offer excellent stability and maneuverability on uneven terrain, making them ideal for challenging environments. Rubber-tired rigs, on the other hand, utilize wheels for mobility, allowing for faster movement on solid surfaces and easier transportation between job sites. Both types cater to diverse drilling applications, balancing mobility, stability, and efficiency for various geological conditions.</p></p>
