<p><p>Wireless communication technologies in healthcare enhance data transfer and connectivity. Bluetooth enables short-range, low-power device communication, ideal for personal health devices. RFID tags allow for efficient tracking of medications and equipment. Ultra Wide Band (UWB) provides precise location data, crucial for patient monitoring. WLAN offers high-speed internet access within healthcare facilities, while WWAN supports broader connectivity for mobile healthcare applications. Wi-Fi enables seamless network access, while Li-Fi utilizes light for data transmission, promising faster speeds and reduced interference in clinical environments.</p></p>
