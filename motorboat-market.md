<p><strong>The Motorboat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outboard Motorboat</li><li>Sterndrive Motorboat</li><li>Inboard Motorboat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorboat market comprises three main types: outboard, sterndrive, and inboard motorboats. Outboard motorboats feature engines mounted externally, providing versatility and ease of maintenance, making them popular for recreational use. Sterndrive motorboats combine an inboard engine with an outboard drive, offering a balance of power and space efficiency. Inboard motorboats have engines housed within the boat's hull, ideal for larger vessels and providing enhanced stability, often favored for cruising and watersports. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences and boating applications.</p></p>
