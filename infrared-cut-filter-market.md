<p><strong>The Infrared Cut Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Infrared Cut Filter</li><li>Absorption Infrared Cut Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared cut filter market is divided into two main types: reflective and absorption filters. Reflective infrared cut filters use thin-film coatings to reflect infrared wavelengths while allowing visible light to pass through, making them ideal for applications like digital cameras and sensors. Absorption infrared cut filters absorb infrared light, preventing it from reaching the imaging sensor, enhancing image quality in optical systems. Both types play crucial roles in improving visibility and accuracy in various imaging applications across industries.</p></p>
