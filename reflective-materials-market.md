<p><ul><li>Micro Glass Bead Type Reflective Materials</li><li>Micro Prismatic Type Reflective Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflective materials market includes two main types: micro glass bead and micro prismatic reflective materials. Micro glass bead type reflective materials utilize tiny glass beads embedded in a surface to reflect light, enhancing visibility in low-light conditions. In contrast, micro prismatic type reflective materials employ a complex prism structure that provides superior reflection and visibility, even at wider angles. Both types are essential in applications like traffic signs, road markings, and safety apparel, contributing to enhanced safety and visibility in various environments.</p></p>
