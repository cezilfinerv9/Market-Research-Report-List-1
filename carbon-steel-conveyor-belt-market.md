<p><ul><li>Plate</li><li>Chained</li><li>Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon steel conveyor belt market can be categorized into three main types: plate, chained, and mesh. Plate belts consist of solid steel plates, offering durability for heavy-duty applications. Chained belts utilize interconnected chains to transport materials, providing flexibility and strength for varied loads. Mesh belts feature a network of interconnected steel wires, ideal for applications requiring air flow and drainage, such as food processing. Each type serves specific industrial needs, leveraging carbon steel's strength and resilience.</p></p>
