<p><p>The external incontinence devices market includes two primary types: disposable catheters and reusable catheters. Disposable catheters are single-use products designed for convenience and hygiene, ideal for patients seeking a low-maintenance option. They are typically made from soft, flexible materials and are discarded after use. In contrast, reusable catheters are designed for multiple uses, requiring proper cleaning and sterilization after each application.
