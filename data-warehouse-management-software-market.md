Standalone systems operate independently, focusing solely on data processing and analysis. ERP modules integrate data warehousing capabilities within broader enterprise resource planning systems, streamlining processes across various business functions. Cloud-based solutions offer scalable, flexible, and cost-effective data management, enabling access from anywhere with internet connectivity. Each type serves distinct organizational needs, enhancing data-driven decision-making and operational efficiency.</p></p>
