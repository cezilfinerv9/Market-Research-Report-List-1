<p><strong>The Gas Powered Truck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CNG Vehicle</li><li>LNG Vehicle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas-powered truck market comprises primarily two types: CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) vehicles and LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) vehicles. CNG vehicles utilize compressed gas stored at high pressure, making them ideal for shorter distances and urban operations due to their immediate fuel availability and lower emissions. LNG vehicles, on the other hand, use liquefied gas stored at cryogenic temperatures, allowing for longer range and higher fuel efficiency, making them suitable for long-haul transportation. Both options contribute to a cleaner alternative to diesel.</p></p>
