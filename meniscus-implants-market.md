<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Synthetic</li><li>Xenograft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meniscus implants market comprises three main types: allografts, synthetic implants, and xenografts. Allografts utilize donor tissue, providing a natural option that closely mimics the original meniscus. Synthetic implants are man-made devices that offer durability and customizable shapes, ideal for various patient needs. Xenografts are derived from animal tissues, presenting an alternative for those who may not respond well to allografts. Each type serves distinct clinical applications, catering to diverse patient profiles in meniscal repair and replacement procedures.</p></p>
