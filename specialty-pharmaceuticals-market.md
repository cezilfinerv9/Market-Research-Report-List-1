<p><p>The specialty pharmaceuticals market comprises various types of drug delivery systems tailored for specific therapeutic needs. Oral pharmaceuticals are administered through the mouth, offering convenience and ease of use, ideal for chronic conditions. Parenteral pharmaceuticals involve injection, providing rapid effects for acute conditions and precise dosage control. Transdermal pharmaceuticals are delivered through the skin, offering sustained drug release and avoiding first-pass metabolism. Together, these delivery methods enhance treatment effectiveness and patient compliance in specialized medical needs.</p></p>
