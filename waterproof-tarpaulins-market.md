<p><p>The waterproof tarpaulin market primarily comprises two types: PVC tarpaulin and PE tarpaulin. PVC tarpaulin is known for its durability and resistance to extreme weather, making it ideal for industrial applications and heavy-duty use. It is also heavier and more expensive. Conversely, PE tarpaulin is lighter, more affordable, and resistant to UV rays, making it suitable for general-purpose use like agriculture and camping. Both materials cater to diverse customer needs, balancing durability, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>
