UASB digesters are efficient for treating high-strength wastewater, while IC reactors enhance mixing and digestion efficiency. EGSB systems are characterized by their ability to handle large volumes and high flow rates. These technologies are pivotal for waste management, energy production, and sustainable resource recovery in various industries.</p></p>
