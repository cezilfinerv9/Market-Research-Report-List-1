<p><ul><li>Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM)</li><li>Synchronous Optical Networking (SONET)</li><li>Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH)</li><li>Fiber Channel</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical data communication market comprises several key types, including Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM), which enables multiple signals on a single optical fiber by using different wavelengths. Synchronous Optical Networking (SONET) and Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH) are standardized protocols for transmitting data over fiber optics, ensuring efficient and reliable communication. Fiber Channel is a high-speed network technology primarily used in storage area networks. Other market segments include passive optical networks and optical transport networks, catering to diverse data transmission needs.</p></p>
