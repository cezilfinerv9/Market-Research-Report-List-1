<p><p>The porous bearing market is categorized into three main types based on their base materials: copper base, iron base, and copper-iron base. Copper base bearings offer excellent wear resistance and low friction characteristics, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Iron base bearings are known for their strength and durability, suitable for heavy-load environments. Copper-iron base bearings combine the benefits of both materials, providing enhanced wear resistance and structural integrity, making them versatile for various industrial applications.</p></p>
