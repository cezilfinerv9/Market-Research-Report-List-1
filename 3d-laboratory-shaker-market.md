<p><ul><li>Tilting</li><li>Rocking</li><li>Orbital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D laboratory shaker market can be categorized into three types: tilting, rocking, and orbital shakers. Tilting shakers provide a back-and-forth motion, ideal for mixing or agitating samples. Rocking shakers utilize a gentle oscillating motion, ensuring even mixing without disturbing sensitive specimens. Orbital shakers, on the other hand, create a circular motion that facilitates uniform mixing. Each type serves specific laboratory applications, enhancing efficiency in sample preparation, biological assays, and other analytical processes across various scientific fields.</p></p>
