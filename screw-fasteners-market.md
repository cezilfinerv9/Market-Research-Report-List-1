<p><strong>The Screw Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Thread Fasteners</li><li>Internal Threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw fasteners market can be categorized into two main types: external thread fasteners and internal threaded fasteners. External thread fasteners, such as screws and bolts, feature threads on the outside, allowing them to be secured into pre-drilled holes or nuts. Internal threaded fasteners, like nuts or inserts, have threads on the inside, enabling them to hold or connect with external thread counterparts. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, ensuring structural integrity and facilitating assembly in diverse applications.</p></p>
