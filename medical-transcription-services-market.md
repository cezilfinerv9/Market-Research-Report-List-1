<p><p>The medical transcription services market comprises two main types: outsourcing and offshoring. Outsourcing involves transferring transcription tasks to external vendors within the same country, allowing healthcare providers to maintain closer communication and quality control. In contrast, offshoring refers to delegating transcription services to companies located in other countries, often driven by cost-efficiency and access to a larger talent pool. Both approaches aim to enhance productivity and reduce costs while ensuring accuracy and compliance with healthcare standards.</p></p>
