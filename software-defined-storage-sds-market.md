<p><p>Software-Defined Storage (SDS) refers to an innovative storage architecture that decouples storage software from the underlying hardware, allowing for flexible resource management and enhanced scalability. This adaptability has made SDS increasingly popular in enterprise IT environments that demand agility, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>The SDS market is experiencing significant growth driven by the rising demand for scalable storage solutions, the proliferation of cloud computing, and the need for optimized data management strategies.
