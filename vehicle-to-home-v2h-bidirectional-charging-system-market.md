<p><p>The Vehicle to Home (V2H) bidirectional charging system allows electric vehicles (EVs) to supply power back to residential systems. Two key standards for V2H charging are CHAdeMo and CCS. CHAdeMo, originating from Japan, supports rapid charging and enables bidirectional energy transfer. In contrast, CCS (Combined Charging System) integrates AC and DC charging, providing flexibility and efficiency. Both systems facilitate renewable energy integration, enhance energy management, and offer backup power during outages, promoting sustainability and resilience in home energy consumption.</p></p>
