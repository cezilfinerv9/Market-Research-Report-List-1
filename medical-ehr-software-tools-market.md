<p><p>Medical EHR software tools are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based EHR systems are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and easier access from various devices, which reduces the need for extensive IT infrastructure. Conversely, on-premise EHR solutions are installed locally on healthcare facilities' servers, granting greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Both types aim to enhance patient care through efficient data management and accessibility.</p></p>
