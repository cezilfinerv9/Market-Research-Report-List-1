<p><strong>The Two-way Control Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-way Globe Valve</li><li>Two-way Butterfly Vavle</li><li>Two-way Ball Vavle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-way control valve market includes three primary types: two-way globe valves, two-way butterfly valves, and two-way ball valves. Two-way globe valves provide precise flow regulation and are ideal for throttling applications. Two-way butterfly valves offer a compact design and quick shut-off capabilities, making them suitable for larger pipelines. Two-way ball valves are known for their excellent sealing and low flow resistance, ensuring efficient on/off control in various industrial applications. Each type serves specific operational needs in fluid control systems.</p></p>
