<p><p>The semiconductor thermal processing furnace market includes various types such as oxidation furnaces, annealing furnaces, and rapid thermal processing (RTP) furnaces. Oxidation furnaces are used to grow thin oxide layers on wafers, enhancing insulation and electronic properties. Annealing furnaces facilitate stress relief and crystallization in materials. RTP furnaces rapidly heat and cool wafers for precise control during processes like doping. Each type plays a critical role in semiconductor fabrication, ensuring optimal material properties and performance in electronic devices.</p></p>
