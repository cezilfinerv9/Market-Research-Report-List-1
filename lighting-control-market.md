<p><p>The lighting control market is categorized into two main types: cable-based and wireless systems. Cable-based controls use physical wiring to manage lighting, offering reliability and low latency, suitable for large installations. Conversely, wireless systems utilize technologies like Zigbee or Wi-Fi, providing flexibility and easier installation, particularly in retrofits. Wireless systems allow for remote control and integration with smart home technologies, appealing to users seeking convenience and advanced features. Both types cater to different user needs and installation contexts.</p></p>
