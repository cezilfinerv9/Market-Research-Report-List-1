<p><p>The microcontroller socket market includes various packaging types such as DIP (Dual In-line Package), BGA (Ball Grid Array), QFP (Quad Flat Package), SOP (Small Outline Package), and SOIC (Small Outline Integrated Circuit). Each type offers distinct characteristics suited to different applications; for example, DIP is popular for through-hole mounting, while BGA provides better thermal performance and density. QFP allows for high pin counts, while SOP and SOIC are favored for surface mounting in compact designs, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
