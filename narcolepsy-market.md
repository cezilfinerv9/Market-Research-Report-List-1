Daytime EDS refers to uncontrollable sleep episodes during the day, significantly affecting daily activities. Cataplexy, a sudden loss of muscle tone triggered by strong emotions, leads to temporary paralysis and can be debilitating. The "Others" category encompasses less common symptoms, such as sleep paralysis and hypnagogic hallucinations. Together, these market segments highlight the diverse challenges faced by individuals with narcolepsy.</p></p>
