<p><ul><li>Tilt Tray Sorter</li><li>Cross Belt Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport automatic baggage sorting systems primarily include tilt tray sorters and cross belt sorters, each designed to enhance baggage handling efficiency. Tilt tray sorters utilize trays that tilt to drop bags at designated destinations, allowing for optimal space utilization and smooth sorting. Cross belt sorters, on the other hand, employ individual belts to transport bags in various directions, facilitating high-speed sorting with minimal damage risk. Both systems aim to streamline operations, reduce wait times, and improve overall passenger experience at airports.</p></p>
