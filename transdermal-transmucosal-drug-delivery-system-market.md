<p><p>The Transdermal and Transmucosal Drug Delivery Systems Market comprises two primary types: the Transdermal Drug Delivery System (TDDS) and the Transmucosal Drug Delivery System (TMDS). TDDS involves delivering drugs through the skin using patches, gels, or ointments, providing a steady release into the bloodstream. Conversely, TMDS administers substances via mucous membranes, such as in the mouth or nose, allowing for rapid absorption and onset of action. Both systems enhance bioavailability and patient compliance while minimizing gastrointestinal side effects.</p></p>
