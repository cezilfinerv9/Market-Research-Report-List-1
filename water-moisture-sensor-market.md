<p><ul><li>Volumetric</li><li>Soil Water Potential</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water moisture sensor market includes two main types: volumetric and soil water potential sensors. Volumetric sensors measure the actual amount of water in the soil, providing direct readings of moisture content, ideal for agricultural and environmental applications. Soil water potential sensors, on the other hand, assess the energy status of water in soil, indicating how easily plants can access moisture. Both types are essential for efficient irrigation management and optimizing water use in various agricultural practices.</p></p>
